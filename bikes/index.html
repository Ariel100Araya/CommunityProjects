How to sell a bike

By Ariel Araya


Step 0: Find a bike 🚲

I didn't think I needed to write that one down... but yet here we are


Step 1: Select the price. 💸

Find a price that is affordable but can still give you a profit


Step 2: Put Bike on front yard. 🏠

Make sure people passing by can see it. if not what is the point of selling it in the first place


Step 3: Wait for people to come by and see it 👀

Let people know its up for offer but also guard it so that it doesn't get stolen


Step 4:If someone comes up to you, discuss pricing 🗣

If agreed on price, trade pieces of paper with value with a glorified metal tube.

If both parties cant agree on a price, say no and go back to step 3.


Step 5: Party 🎉

Congrats you just sold a bike
